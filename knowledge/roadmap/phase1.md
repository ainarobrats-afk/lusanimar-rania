# Roadmap Phase 1

Phase 1 focuses on building the core knowledge foundation for travel and Timor-Leste.

## Goals

- Standardize and complete knowledge for Timor-Leste, NTT, Indonesia.
- Build airport intelligence knowledge for common passenger questions.
- Add arrival assistant content for Dili Airport.
- Prepare diaspora and country knowledge for key markets.

## Deliverables

- `knowledge/airport_guide/`
- `knowledge/timor_leste/`
- `knowledge/ntt/`
- `knowledge/indonesia/`
- `knowledge/diaspora/`
- `knowledge/templates/`
