# Embassy Help

This guide explains how to get assistance from your embassy or consulate.

## When to contact your embassy

- Lost passport or travel documents.
- Emergency medical situations.
- Legal problems or arrest.
- Natural disasters or civil unrest.
- Need assistance with family emergencies.

## What information to provide

- Full name and passport number.
- Current location and contact details.
- Description of the emergency.
- Any police or medical reports.

## What embassies can do

- Provide guidance on local procedures.
- Help replace passports or emergency documents.
- Assist with contacting family members.
- Provide a list of local lawyers or medical services.

## Important notes

- Embassies cannot pay your bills or provide money.
- Keep all communication records with the embassy.
- Follow embassy advice closely for your safety.
