# Medical Help

This guide explains how to find medical assistance while traveling.

## What to do in a medical emergency

- Seek immediate medical attention at a nearby hospital or clinic.
- Call local emergency services if the situation is serious.
- Keep your passport and travel insurance information with you.

## Finding medical services

- Ask airport staff or hotel staff for the nearest medical facility.
- Use trusted local resources or embassy guidance.
- Prefer clinics with English-speaking staff when possible.

## Travel insurance

- Present your travel insurance details at the medical facility.
- Keep all medical bills and reports for insurance claims.
- Contact your insurer as soon as possible.

## Tips

- Carry a list of any medicines and allergies.
- Keep a copy of your medical and insurance information in your phone.
- If you need prescription medication, ask the facility for a written note.
