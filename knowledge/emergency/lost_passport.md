# Lost Passport

This guide helps travelers who lose their passport while abroad.

## What to do immediately

- Stay calm and stop at a safe location.
- Report the loss to local police and get a copy of the report.
- Contact your embassy or consulate.
- Do not leave the area until you have documented the loss.

## Documents to prepare

- Police report or incident report.
- Identification if available (driver license, national ID).
- Travel itinerary and flight ticket.
- Proof of citizenship if available.

## Next steps

- Visit the nearest embassy or consulate.
- Request an emergency travel document or temporary passport.
- Follow the embassy instructions for the application process.
- Keep receipts and copies of all documents.
