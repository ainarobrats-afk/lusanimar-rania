# Baggage Claim

This guide explains how to collect checked baggage after landing.

## After landing

- Follow signs to baggage claim or arrivals.
- Check the baggage carousel number on the display screens.
- Wait at the correct carousel for your flight.
- Identify your luggage by the tag, color, and shape.

## If your baggage is delayed or missing

- Wait until the carousel stops and no more bags appear.
- Go to the baggage services or lost luggage counter.
- Provide your boarding pass and baggage claim ticket.
- Describe your bag clearly and give any special details.
- Keep the reference number until you receive your bag.

## Tips

- Attach a unique ribbon or sticker to your bag.
- Keep essential items in your carry-on.
- Be patient; baggage may take 15–30 minutes after landing.
