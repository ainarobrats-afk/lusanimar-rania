# Flight Delay

This guide explains what to do when a flight is delayed.

## First steps

- Check the departure screen for updated flight status.
- Confirm the delay reason with airline staff.
- Keep your boarding pass and contact details ready.

## Your rights

- Ask the airline about meal vouchers and refreshment options.
- If the delay is long, ask about rebooking or accommodation.
- Keep receipts for any expenses related to the delay.

## What to ask the airline

- When is the new departure time?
- Will my connecting flight be rebooked?
- Is a meal or refreshment voucher available?
- Will accommodation be provided for overnight delays?

## Tips

- Stay near the airline desk or gate for announcements.
- Use the airline app for real-time updates.
- Keep calm and ask politely for the next steps.
