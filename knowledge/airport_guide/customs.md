# Customs

This guide explains customs rules and what passengers must declare.

## Customs channels

- Green channel: nothing to declare.
- Red channel: goods or cash to declare.
- Follow airport signs to the correct channel.

## When to declare

- Cash or currency over the local declaration limit.
- High-value goods or gifts above the duty-free allowance.
- Food, plants, medicines, or animal products that may be restricted.
- Items for commercial use or resale.

## Common customs rules

- Most countries require declaration of cash above USD 10,000 or equivalent.
- Declare goods honestly; undeclared goods may be confiscated.
- Keep receipts and invoices for expensive items.

## Restricted items

- Fresh fruit, vegetables, meat, and plants may be prohibited.
- Some medicines require prescriptions or special permits.
- Weapons, sharp objects, and flammable items are usually banned.

## Tips

- If in doubt, declare the item.
- Keep receipts for purchases and cash amounts.
- Follow customs officer instructions.
