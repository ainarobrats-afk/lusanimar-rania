# Check-In

This guide explains how to check in for your flight both online and at the airport.

## Online check-in

- Opens 24–48 hours before departure.
- Visit the airline website or mobile app.
- Enter your booking reference (PNR) and last name.
- Confirm your passenger details and select a seat.
- Download or save your boarding pass to your phone.
- Print a copy if you prefer a physical boarding pass.
- If you have checked baggage, go to the bag drop counter at the airport.

## Self check-in kiosk

1. Find your airline kiosk or common-use kiosk.
2. Identify yourself using one of these options:
   - Scan passport
   - Scan QR code from booking email
   - Enter booking reference and last name
3. Confirm your flight and passenger details.
4. Select or confirm your seat.
5. Print your boarding pass.
6. Attach baggage tags if required.
7. Take the boarding pass to the bag drop counter.

## Airport check-in counter

- If you still need to check in at the counter, bring your passport and booking details.
- The staff can help with seat selection, baggage, and special requests.

## Key reminders

- Keep your boarding pass handy until boarding is complete.
- Check-in closes at least 60 minutes before international departure and earlier for some airlines.
- If you have only carry-on luggage, you may go directly to security after check-in.
- For connecting flights, verify whether you must re-check baggage.
