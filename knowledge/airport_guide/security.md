# Security

This guide covers airport security screening and what passengers should prepare.

## Security screening steps

- Have boarding pass and passport ready.
- Place your carry-on bag on the conveyor belt.
- Put liquids in a transparent bag (100 ml maximum per container, 1 liter total).
- Place laptops, tablets, and electronics in a separate tray.
- Remove jackets, belts, watches, and metal items.
- Remove shoes if required by the airport.
- Walk through the security scanner when instructed.
- Collect your belongings from the trays and repack them calmly.

## Liquids and restricted items

- Liquids, gels, and aerosols must be in containers no larger than 100 ml.
- All containers must fit in one clear, resealable 1 liter bag.
- Do not carry sharp objects, flammable items, or weapons in cabin baggage.
- Common prohibited items: scissors, knives, lighters, large batteries, and aerosols.

## Electronic devices

- Remove laptops and tablets from bags.
- Place them in a separate tray for screening.
- Keep phones and small electronics in your carry-on bag unless instructed.

## Tips for faster security

- Wear slip-on shoes.
- Keep your passport and boarding pass accessible.
- Pack liquids and electronics separately.
- Be ready to remove jackets and belts.
- Follow instructions from security officers.
