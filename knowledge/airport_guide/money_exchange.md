# Money Exchange

This guide explains how to handle currency exchange at the airport.

## Currency exchange options

- Airport money changers are convenient but often have lower rates.
- Airport ATMs usually offer better rates than currency counters.
- Choose the local currency option at ATMs to avoid dynamic conversion fees.

## What to bring

- Local currency for immediate expenses.
- A debit or credit card with international withdrawal capability.
- A small amount of major currency such as USD if needed.

## Tips

- Withdraw only what you need for the first day or two.
- Avoid changing large amounts at the airport if possible.
- Compare rates quickly between ATM and money changer.
- Keep receipts for all currency exchanges.
