# SIM Card

This guide explains how to get mobile internet at the airport.

## Buying a local SIM card

- Many airports have SIM card shops or kiosks in arrivals.
- Bring your passport for registration.
- Choose a plan that matches your travel duration and data needs.
- Ask for help to install the SIM if needed.

## eSIM options

- eSIM can be purchased before travel and activated on arrival.
- eSIM is useful if you do not want a physical card.
- Check that your phone supports eSIM.

## Airport vs city SIM

- Airport SIM cards are convenient but sometimes more expensive.
- City stores may offer lower prices and more choice.
- For immediate connectivity, use airport SIM and compare rates later.

## Tips

- Keep your home number active in one slot if possible.
- Save the SIM provider name and customer service number.
- Use a local number for easy local communication.
