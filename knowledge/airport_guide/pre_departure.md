# Pre-Departure

This guide helps passengers prepare before they leave for the airport.

## Checklist before departure

- Confirm flight schedule and terminal.
- Complete online check-in when available (usually 24–48 hours before departure).
- Save boarding pass on your phone and print a copy if needed.
- Verify visa and travel documents for your destination.
- Check passport validity (minimum 6 months required by many countries).
- Keep passport, ticket, visa, and hotel booking in one travel folder.
- Review baggage rules and allowances for your airline.
- Prepare a small carry-on bag with essentials: phone, charger, wallet, medicines.
- Pack liquids in containers of 100 ml or less, in one transparent 1 liter bag.
- Keep important documents in your carry-on, not in checked baggage.

## Before leaving home

- Confirm transportation to the airport.
- Check traffic, weather, and airline updates.
- Charge electronic devices and power banks.
- Note emergency contacts and arrival address in the destination city.
- Consider travel insurance and digital backups of documents.

## At departure point

- Arrive early: international flights 3 hours before, domestic flights 90 minutes before.
- Have passport and boarding pass ready.
- Keep your boarding group and gate information in easy reach.
- Confirm the airport terminal and airline counter.

## Tips

- Use a luggage scale at home to avoid overweight fees.
- Keep a photo of your passport and ticket in case of loss.
- If you have checked baggage, allow extra time for bag drop.
- Print or save your itinerary and accommodation details.
