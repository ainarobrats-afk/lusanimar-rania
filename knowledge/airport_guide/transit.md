# Transit

This guide explains airport transit and layover procedures.

## Transit basics

- If you have a connecting flight, follow signs for "Transfers" or "Connecting Flights."
- Stay inside the secure area unless you must pass through immigration.
- Check your next flight gate and departure time.
- Allow at least 90 minutes for international connections and 60 minutes for domestic connections.

## Transit steps

1. Arrive at the transfer area.
2. Check the screens for your next flight and gate.
3. Complete transit security screening if required.
4. Proceed to the gate area and wait for boarding.

## Transit visa

- Some countries require a transit visa even if you do not leave the airport.
- Check visa requirements before travel.
- If you must enter the country during a long layover, follow immigration procedures.

## Tips

- Keep your boarding pass and passport ready.
- Monitor gate changes and announcements.
- Stay near your departure gate if your layover is short.
- Confirm whether your baggage is checked through to the final destination.
