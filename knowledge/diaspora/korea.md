# Diaspora Korea — Timor-Leste

Supported languages: tet / id / en / pt / zh

## Community Overview
- Seoul, Incheon, Busan.
- Important for EPS workers and student communities.

## community_size
- Seoul/Incheon: medium-large
- Busan: small-medium

## popular_jobs
- manufacturing
- construction
- shipbuilding
- hospitality
- IT services

## nearest_airports
- Seoul: ICN, GMP
- Busan: PUS

## money_transfer
- Use international bank transfer or remittance services.
- Compare fees and exchange rates for KRW to USD/IDR.
- Some Korean banks offer special transfer services for migrant workers.

## emergency_contacts
- Emergency numbers: 112 or 119
- For foreign worker assistance, contact local support centers.
- Keep permission to stay and work documents ready.

## travel_routes_to_dili
- ICN → CGK → DIL or ICN → DPS → DIL.
- Busan: PUS → ICN/GMP → CGK/DPS → DIL.

## EPS worker information
- Program EPS menyediakan izin kerja dan pelatihan pra-keberangkatan.
- Pekerja perlu memahami hak kerja, upah minimum, dan perpanjangan izin.
- Informasi lebih lanjut ada di kantor tenaga kerja Korea Selatan dan agen resmi.
