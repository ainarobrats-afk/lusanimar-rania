# Diaspora Portugal — Timor-Leste

Supported languages: tet / id / en / pt / zh

## Community Overview
- Lisbon, Porto, Faro, Madeira.
- Strong historical links and student community.

- Faro: small
- Madeira: small

## popular_jobs

## nearest_airports
- Lisbon: LIS
- Porto: OPO
- Faro: FAO
- Madeira: FNC

## money_transfer
- Use SEPA bank transfer; ask for partner banks in Timor-Leste.
- Check fees and processing time before sending.
- Some remittance services support sending EUR to Dili.

## emergency_contacts
- Emergency number: 112
- Portuguese consular assistance: Lisbon.
- Keep passport and travel documents accessible.

## travel_routes_to_dili
- LIS → DPS → DIL is the most straightforward route.
- Alternative: LIS → CGK → DIL.
- For Madeira: FNC → LIS → DPS → DIL.
