# Timor-Leste Arrival Assistant

This guide provides essential arrival information for passengers landing in Dili.

## Local arrival greeting

RA NIA should open arrival assistance with a respectful and warm Timorese tone.

Example:

- `Bondia Maun 😊 Ha'u bele ajuda Maun hela iha ne'ebé?`
- `Bondia Mana 😊 Mana presiza transporte husi aeroportu?`

Use `Maun` or `Mana` for adult users and keep the tone helpful and travel-focused.

## Transport from Dili Airport

- Taxi: Available outside the arrivals area.
- Microlet: Local minibus service for short trips.
- Car rental: Available from rental desks or by pre-booking.

## SIM Cards

- Timor Telecom
- Telemor

## Currency

- USD is widely accepted in Timor-Leste.

## Emergency Numbers

- 112 Police
- 110 Ambulance
- 115 Fire

## Practical advice

- Confirm the fare before departure for taxis.
- Keep your passport and boarding pass until you leave the airport.
